Design System - OverView

A design system is a set of standards and guidelines that manage design at scale by reducing redundancy while creating a shared language and visual consistency. It is a collection of reusable components, patterns, styles, and guidelines that aid in streamlining and optimizing design efforts. Design systems can include pattern libraries, design languages, style guides, coded components, brand languages, and documentation. They are designed to help digital product design and development of products such as applications or websites.

Overall, creating a design system is an iterative process that requires collaboration and communication among team members. It's important to be flexible and willing to make changes as needed to ensure that your design system meets your organization's needs and goals.
